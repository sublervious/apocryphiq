Apocryphiq — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Apocryphiq ("the App") is a personal desktop application operated by a single individual. This policy describes what data the App handles and how.

1. No Third-Party Users

The App is not distributed to or used by anyone other than its operator. It does not collect, store, or process personal data from any third party.

2. Data the App Handles

The App stores the following data locally on the operator's own computer:

  • API credentials for services the operator subscribes to (Anthropic, ElevenLabs, Fal.ai, TikTok)
  • Video scripts, images, audio, and rendered videos generated by the App
  • OAuth tokens for the operator's own TikTok account

No data is transmitted to any server operated by the App itself — the App has no backend. Data is sent only to the third-party APIs listed above, under their respective privacy policies, for the sole purpose of generating and publishing content.

3. TikTok Data

Through TikTok's Login Kit and Content Posting API, the App accesses only the operator's own account information (display name, avatar) and posting capability. It does not access other users' data, followers' data, or any direct messages. TikTok data is never shared with any other party and can be deleted at any time by removing the App's access at TikTok settings.

4. Analytics

The App may retrieve view and engagement metrics for videos posted by the operator's own account, used solely to inform the operator's content decisions. These metrics are stored locally.

5. Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.

6. Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to the operator at nathanadamwilson@gmail.com.
