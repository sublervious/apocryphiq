What is Apocryphiq?

Apocryphiq is a desktop application that researches, writes, narrates, and assembles short-form storytelling videos about ancient religious texts — the Ethiopian Bible, the books of Enoch and Jubilees, the Dead Sea Scrolls, and other writings that were left out of the modern biblical canon. Finished videos are published to the @apocryphiq TikTok channel.

How it works

1 · ResearchAncient texts and historical context become a script
2 · NarrateA voiceover brings the story to life
3 · IllustrateScenes are visualized as cinematic imagery
4 · PublishThe finished video is posted to TikTok

Personal project

Apocryphiq is a personal application operated by a single creator. It is not distributed for download or available to third parties — it connects only to the operator's own TikTok account.
