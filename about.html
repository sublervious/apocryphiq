About Apocryphiq

✦

Apocryphiq began with a simple question: why does the Ethiopian Bible contain twenty-two books that mine did not? That question opened a door into a vast, half-forgotten library — the Book of Enoch quoted in the New Testament yet cut from most Bibles, the Book of Jubilees retelling Genesis with secret detail, the three Ethiopian Meqabyan found nowhere else on Earth. These texts were copied, treasured, and read as scripture for centuries before councils and history set them aside.

Apocryphiq exists to bring those writings back into the light — not as a scholar's lecture, but as short, vivid stories anyone can follow. Each one is carefully researched from the source texts and their history, then retold with narration and original illustration.

Our Approach

Faithful to the source

Every story we tell is grounded in the actual ancient text and the scholarship around it. We don't invent scripture; we surface what is already written and explain where it comes from, how it survived, and why it was left out of the modern canon. Where traditions differ, we say so.

Our aim is reverent curiosity — to treat these writings as the serious, strange, and beautiful documents they are, and to make them accessible to a generation that has never heard of them.

Who We Are

A personal studio

Apocryphiq is an independent project, created and operated by a single person. It is not a company, a church, or an academic institution — just one researcher's effort to share these texts with a wider audience through short-form video.
