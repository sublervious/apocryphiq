The Project

What is Apocryphiq?

For sixteen centuries, the Ethiopian Orthodox Church kept eighty-eight books in its Bible while the rest of the world trimmed theirs to sixty-six. Apocryphiq is dedicated to the difference — the removed scrolls, the apocryphal gospels, the prophecies of Enoch, and the ancient accounts of angels, giants, and hidden history that never made the final cut. We research these texts, retell them faithfully, and publish them as short documentary videos for a modern audience who never knew they existed.
