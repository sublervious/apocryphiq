What is the Ethiopian Bible?

The Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church holds the broadest biblical canon in the world — around 88 books, compared to the 66 in most Protestant Bibles. It preserves texts the rest of Christianity set aside, including the Book of Enoch and the Book of Jubilees.

Are these texts "real" scripture?

They are real, ancient documents — copied, read, and treated as scripture by major traditions for many centuries. Whether a given book is canonical depends on which tradition you ask. Apocryphiq presents what the texts say and where they stand, rather than ruling on doctrine.

Where does Apocryphiq get its information?

Each story is researched from the source texts themselves and the historical scholarship surrounding them. We aim to retell faithfully and to note where traditions and translations differ.

How are the videos made?

Every video follows the same four steps: research into a script, narration, original illustration of each scene, and final assembly. The result is a short documentary-style piece you can watch in a minute or two.
